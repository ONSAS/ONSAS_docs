Principle of Virtual Thermal Work

Variational formulation of the heat flow equation

\[ \int_{\Omega} k \nabla T \cdot \nabla \delta T dV + \int_{\Omega} \rho c \frac{\partial T}{\partial t} \delta T dV = \int_{\Omega} Q_h \delta T d V + \int_{\partial \Omega} k \nabla T \cdot \hat{\mathbf{n}} \delta T d S \qquad \forall \delta T \in \tilde{\mathcal{T}}\]

Considering the boundary conditions

\[\left\{ \begin{array}{lr} T(\mathbf{x},t) = f_D(t) & \text{on} \,\Gamma_D \\ -k \frac{\partial T}{\partial n} (\mathbf{x},t) = f_N(\mathbf{x},t) & \text{on} \, \Gamma_N \\ -k \frac{\partial T}{\partial n} (\mathbf{x},t) = h \left( T(\mathbf{x},t)-T_\infty(t) \right) & \text{on} \, \Gamma_R \end{array} \right.\]

where $h$ is the convection coefficient and $T_\infty(t)$ is the ambient temperature at time $t$.

\[ \int_{\Omega} k \nabla T \cdot \nabla \delta T dV + \int_{\Omega} \rho c \frac{\partial T}{\partial t} \delta T dV + \int_{\Gamma_R} h T(\mathbf{x},t) \delta T d S = \int_{\Omega} Q_h \delta T d V + \int_{\Gamma_N} q_{inp}(\mathbf{x},t) \delta T d S + \int_{\Gamma_R} h T_\infty(t) \delta T d S \qquad \forall \delta T \in \tilde{\mathcal{T}}\]

where $q_{inp}$ is the input heat flow $q_{inp} = -f_N$.
